More Questions?

How long does delivery take?

+

We typically process orders within 1-2 business days. Delivery across Tamil Nadu takes 3-5 days, and nationwide shipping takes 5-7 business days.

Do you provide installation services?

+

We primarily provide premium DIY kits and high-quality standalone products that are very easy to assemble and set up yourself for any celebration.

Do you ship nationwide?

+

Yes, we ship our premium celebration supplies all across India. We ensure secure packaging for fragile items like brass lamps and neon signs.

Can I customize the product packages?

+

Absolutely! You can mix and match items or contact our team to create a custom bundle that perfectly fits your specific celebration requirements.

Do you offer custom birthday themes and neon signs?

+

Yes, we specialize in custom birthday setups! From personalized neon name signs to specific color-themed balloon arches, we can curate a unique look for your special day.
